About Me

Personal Journey

Hello! My name is Moshe Nussbaum, and I am a veteran Israeli journalist known for my role as the senior crime and police affairs correspondent at Channel 12 News.

In my personal life, I am married and a proud father of three. I enjoy following sports, especially soccer and basketball, and have a strong passion for Israeli music. I also like spending quality time with family and friends. One of the most influential people in my life has been Ilana Dayan, a fellow journalist who has always inspired me with her investigative reporting and journalistic integrity.

In 2022, I was diagnosed with ALS, a neurodegenerative disease that has challenged me physically but has not stopped me from continuing to work and inspire others. I believe in facing life with resilience, dignity, and humor.

Career Highlights

I began my professional journey in the Israel Defense Forces, serving as a paratrooper and later as a tank commander. After completing my military service, I joined the Israel Police, where I served as a detective and eventually transitioned into journalism.

My media career started with radio reporting at Kol Israel, and I later became a prominent figure in television journalism when I joined Channel 12 News in 1993. Over the years, I have covered major criminal investigations and political scandals in Israeli history. My deep understanding of police work, combined with my calm and professional delivery, has made me a respected and trusted journalist.

Q&A

מהם שלושה הבדלים עיקריים בין Web2, Web1 ו-Web3?

  • Web1: אינטרנט טקסטואלי בלבד – ניתן רק לקרוא תוכן.
  • Web2: אינטרנט אינטראקטיבי – המשתמשים יוצרים ומשתפים תוכן.
  • Web3: אינטרנט חכם ואישי – מערכות לומדות את המשתמש ומציעות תוכן מותאם.

What are three main differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3?

  • Web1: read-only internet: users could view content only.
  • Web2: interactive internet: users create and share content.
  • Web3: smart internet: systems adapt to user needs using AI.

תרחיש שימוש: חיפוש שיר ששכחת את שמו

  • Web1: אתר סטטי עם רשימות שירים לפי עשורים.
  • Web2: שאלות בפורומים או רשתות חברתיות כמו Reddit.
  • Web3: בינה מלאכותית מזהה את השיר ומציעה שירים דומים.

Use Case: Searching for a forgotten song

  • Web1: A static lyrics site listing songs by decade.
  • Web2: Asking in music forums or social platforms like Reddit.
  • Web3: AI matches your humming and finds the song, even suggesting similar ones.
